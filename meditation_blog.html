5-Minute Meditations

Short, effective resets you can do between classes or meetings.

Why 5-Minute Meditations Work

Even a few minutes of mindful breathing or guided meditation can help calm your mind, lower stress levels, and improve focus. These quick practices are perfect for busy schedules where longer meditation sessions may feel overwhelming.

Simple Techniques You Can Try

1. Box Breathing: Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4. Repeat for 5 minutes.
2. Body Scan: Gently bring awareness to each part of your body, starting from your toes up to your head. Notice sensations without judgment.
3. Visualization: Close your eyes and imagine a calming place — the beach, a forest, or anywhere peaceful. Spend 5 minutes immersed in the details of this space.

Benefits You’ll Notice

  • Reduced stress and tension
  • Improved concentration
  • Better emotional balance
  • Quick recharge for mental energy
